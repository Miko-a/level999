# Beginner Guide

Source ID: beginner-guide
Topic: beginner basics
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo

Honkai: Star Rail is a turn-based RPG where players build teams of characters. Each character has a Path, Element, Light Cone, Relics, Traces, and role in battle.

For beginners, the most important priorities are:

1. Build one main damage dealer first.
2. Keep one sustain character in the team, such as a healer or shielder.
3. Level up characters, Light Cones, and important Traces before farming perfect Relics.
4. Do not spend too many resources trying to get perfect Relic substats early.
5. Use a balanced team instead of using only damage dealers.

A simple beginner team usually contains:

- One main DPS
- One support
- One sustain
- One flexible character

If a player is new, they should focus on story progression, character level, Light Cone level, and Trace upgrades before deep endgame optimization.
