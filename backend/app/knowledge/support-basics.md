# Support Basics

Source ID: support-basics
Category: role
Topic: support basics
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo
Tags: support, harmony, nihility, buffer, debuffer, beginner

Support characters improve team performance. They may increase damage, speed, energy, survivability, or apply debuffs to enemies.

Common support functions:

- Buffing ally damage
- Increasing speed or action value
- Restoring energy
- Applying debuffs
- Reducing enemy defense or resistance
- Improving team consistency

Beginner advice:

1. Do not ignore support characters.
2. A well-built support can increase total team damage significantly.
3. Supports usually do not need perfect offensive Relics early.
4. Speed and energy-related stats are often useful for supports.
5. Upgrade support abilities that improve the whole team.

A team with one strong DPS and good support is usually better than a team with four underbuilt DPS characters.

Support characters are especially important in harder content where raw damage alone is not enough.
