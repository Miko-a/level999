# Team Building Basics

Source ID: team-building-basics
Topic: team building
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo

A basic Honkai: Star Rail team usually has four characters. A stable beginner team commonly includes:

1. Main DPS
2. Support or buffer
3. Sustain character
4. Flexible slot

The main DPS is responsible for most of the team's damage.

A support or buffer helps the team by increasing damage, speed, energy, or other useful combat effects.

A sustain character keeps the team alive. Sustain characters may heal, shield, reduce damage, or protect allies.

The flexible slot can be used for a second damage dealer, debuffer, extra support, or another sustain if the content is difficult.

Beginner team-building rules:

- Do not use four DPS characters in one team unless the content is very easy.
- Always bring a sustain character when survival is a problem.
- Match the team to enemy weakness when possible.
- Build a small number of characters first instead of spreading resources too thin.
- A weaker but well-built team is usually better than many underbuilt characters.

If a player keeps dying, they should improve sustain, character levels, Light Cone levels, and defensive stats.
