# Survival Guide

Source ID: survival-guide
Category: combat
Topic: survival
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo
Tags: survival, dying, sustain, healer, shielder, defense, beginner

If a player keeps dying in Honkai: Star Rail, the problem is usually related to sustain, levels, defensive stats, or team structure.

Common reasons a team dies:

1. No sustain character in the team.
2. Sustain character is underleveled.
3. Light Cone is underleveled.
4. Important defensive or healing Traces are not upgraded.
5. Relics have poor main stats.
6. The team uses too many damage dealers and not enough support.
7. The enemy weakness is ignored.

A safer beginner team should include:

- One main DPS
- One support
- One sustain
- One flexible character

A sustain character may be a healer, shielder, or defensive utility character.

If survival is the problem, improve these first:

1. Character level
2. Sustain character level
3. Sustain Light Cone level
4. Important sustain Traces
5. Relic main stats such as HP, Defense, Speed, or Outgoing Healing when appropriate

Beginner advice:

If a battle feels impossible, do not only increase damage. Improving sustain and basic progression can be more effective.
