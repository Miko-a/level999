# Relic Basics

Source ID: relic-basics
Topic: relics
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo

Relics are equipment pieces that give characters additional stats. Relics are important, but beginners should not over-focus on perfect Relics too early.

There are two broad equipment groups:

1. Cavern Relics
2. Planar Ornaments

Cavern Relics usually provide 2-piece and 4-piece set effects. Planar Ornaments usually provide 2-piece effects.

Important Relic concepts:

- Main stat is usually more important than substats for beginners.
- Correct main stats can make a character much more usable.
- Perfect substats are mostly important for late-game optimization.
- Early players should use usable Relics instead of waiting for perfect ones.
- Relic farming becomes more valuable after the player has reached higher progression levels.

General beginner advice:

- Upgrade character level first.
- Upgrade Light Cone level.
- Upgrade important Traces.
- Then improve Relics gradually.

For DPS characters, common useful stats include Crit Rate, Crit Damage, Attack, Speed, or Elemental Damage depending on the character.

For sustain characters, useful stats may include HP, Defense, Outgoing Healing, Effect RES, or Speed depending on the character.
