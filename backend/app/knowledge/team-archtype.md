# Team Archetypes

Source ID: team-archetypes
Category: team
Topic: team archetypes
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo
Tags: team, archetype, dps, support, sustain, beginner

A team archetype is a general structure for building a team.

Basic beginner archetype:

- Main DPS
- Support
- Sustain
- Flexible slot

Double support archetype:

- Main DPS
- Support
- Support
- Sustain

This structure improves the main DPS by giving buffs, debuffs, or energy support.

Double sustain archetype:

- Main DPS
- Support
- Sustain
- Sustain

This structure is useful when the player keeps dying, but it may reduce total damage.

Dual DPS archetype:

- Main DPS
- Sub DPS
- Support
- Sustain

This structure can work when both damage dealers contribute without consuming too many resources.

Beginner recommendation:

Use the basic beginner archetype first. It is stable, easy to understand, and works well while learning the game.

Common mistake:

Using four damage dealers often creates an unstable team. The team may lack healing, shielding, buffs, debuffs, or skill point balance.
