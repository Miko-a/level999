# Damage Dealer Basics

Source ID: damage-dealer-basics
Category: role
Topic: damage dealer basics
Game: Honkai: Star Rail
Version: internal-demo
Tags: dps, damage dealer, crit, attack, speed, beginner

A damage dealer, often called DPS, is responsible for most of the team's damage.

Beginner DPS priorities:

1. Level the DPS character.
2. Level the DPS Light Cone.
3. Upgrade important damage Traces.
4. Use Relics with useful main stats.
5. Improve Relic substats later.

Useful DPS stats often include:

- Attack
- Crit Rate
- Crit Damage
- Speed
- Elemental Damage

The exact best stats depend on the character's kit, but beginners should first focus on usable main stats instead of perfect substats.

Common DPS mistakes:

- Building too many DPS characters at once.
- Ignoring Light Cone level.
- Farming Relics too early.
- Using a team with no sustain.
- Ignoring support characters.

A strong DPS still needs a good team. Support and sustain characters can make the DPS perform better and survive longer.
